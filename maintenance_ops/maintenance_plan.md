# Preventive Maintenance Workflow

## Objective
Turn the spreadsheet-driven PM process into a repeatable maintenance program.

## Lifecycle
1. Define maintenance program by asset category.
2. Attach checklist tasks and target frequency.
3. Generate work orders for applicable outlets/assets.
4. Mark work orders due and overdue by date.
5. Complete work orders with checklist results.
6. Close and archive the record.

## What the system should calculate
- next due date
- overdue flag
- priority
- assignee based on outlet city and team routing

## What the system should capture
- actual completion date
- checklist results
- failed items
- remarks
- technician notes

## Operational rule
Preventive maintenance should never be stored as ad hoc rows per outlet.
It should be generated from a reusable template so that a policy change affects all applicable outlets.
